You can perform an interview in a matter of minutes. Who is an important
person in your area of interest that you believe would spark the interest of
your readers?

Discuss with this person current events or get their ideas of what’s coming next
in your industry. The goal here isn’t just to have an interview, but
to talk about things that your readers want to know about.

It could be business insights, lessons they’ve learnt, sharing their expertise, etc.
